About GPhysics

GPhysics is designed to make physics accessible, exciting, and empowering for high school girls around the world. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of women physicists by providing opportunities for learning, collaboration, and competition.

Competition Details

GPhysics offers Classical Mechanics problems at varying difficulty levels, along with exciting collaborative team rounds. Competition Date: November 2025. Stay tuned for official registration details and prizes!

Registration

Registration opens August 2025. Please check back then!

Competition Rules

  • Participants must be high school girls (grades 9–12) who are U.S. citizens or green card holders.
  • Individual rounds must be completed without external assistance.
  • Team rounds allow collaboration within registered teams.
  • Participants must register individually and optionally form teams.
