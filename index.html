What is GPhysics?

GPhysics is a free, online competition designed to make physics exciting, approachable, and empowering for girls in grades 6–8. Through fun challenges, creative problem-solving, and collaboration, young participants will explore physics and hopefully be inspired.

Key Features

  • Entirely free and online — open worldwide
  • Tailored specifically for middle school girls (grades 6–8)
  • Includes individual, team, and relay-style rounds
  • Workshops and mentorship opportunities with female scientists
  • Fun awards and digital certificates for all
