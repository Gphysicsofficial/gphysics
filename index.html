Welcome to GPhysics!

A free, online physics competition designed to spark a passion for physics among high school girls worldwide. Through challenging problems and real-world applications, GPhysics offers an exciting and empowering experience.

About GPhysics

GPhysics is an initiative founded to inspire high school girls to explore the beauty of physics and scientific thinking. We aim to foster a welcoming and accessible community through competitions, workshops, and collaborative teamwork.

Competition Details

Subject Track: Classical Mechanics

Timeline: Registration opens August 2025. Competition date: November 2025 (exact date coming soon).

Prizes: Certificates for all participants, awards for top scorers, potential Amazon gift cards and mentorship opportunities depending on sponsorships.

Competition Rules

  • Participants must be high school girls (grades 9–12) who are U.S. citizens or green card holders.
  • All individual rounds must be completed individually without external assistance.
  • Team rounds encourage collaborative teamwork with registered team members only.
  • Winners must provide a valid shipping address to receive prizes.
  • By registering, participants consent to competition updates via email.
