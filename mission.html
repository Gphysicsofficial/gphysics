Our Mission

GPhysics exists to break down barriers in science — starting with physics.

We’re a student-led initiative focused on bringing physics to middle school girls in a way that’s fun, inclusive, and meaningful. Too often, physics feels distant or intimidating. Our mission is to make it creative, hands-on, and empowering — something anyone can explore and enjoy.

We’ve seen what happens when girls feel confident in STEM: they speak up, ask big questions, and lead bold experiments. That’s why we’ve built GPhysics as more than just a competition — it’s a space to try, to collaborate, and to grow together.

We Believe In:

  • 💡 Learning through curiosity and exploration, not just grades or tests
  • 🌍 Accessibility — all events are free and 100% online
  • 🤝 Collaboration — science is better with teamwork and discussion
  • 🎙 Representation — hearing from real women in physics and STEM
  • 🎨 Creativity — problem solving should be imaginative, not rigid

Whether you dream of discovering dark matter or just love building things that roll, you belong here. GPhysics is your space to explore, ask questions, and see how far your curiosity can take you.
