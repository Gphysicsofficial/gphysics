Competition Overview

GPhysics is designed to challenge, inspire, and excite high school girls with a passion for physics. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or just beginning to explore the world of science, GPhysics offers a welcoming space for you to stretch your mind and grow your skills.

Tracks

In 2025, GPhysics focuses on Classical Mechanics — the foundation of physics. Expect thought-provoking conceptual questions, real-world problem applications, and challenging calculation-based exercises that test not just memorization, but true understanding.

Competition Structure

  • Individual Round: Solve a set of conceptual and calculation problems independently. You choose the Classical Mechanics track when you register!
  • Team Round: Work with up to 3 teammates to solve open-ended, real-world inspired physics challenges collaboratively.
  • Guts Round: A fast-paced, multi-topic round where speed and teamwork are key. Problems get harder as time runs out!

Prizes

  • Top Individual Scorers: Amazon gift cards + certificates
  • Best Team Collaboration Award: Amazon gift cards
  • Most Creative Solution Award (for Team Round): Amazon gift cards
  • Certificates for all participants

Depending on sponsorships, select winners may also receive small scholarships or mentorship opportunities with women physicists!

Why Participate?

GPhysics is not just about winning — it's about discovery, creativity, and community. Every participant joins a network of young women passionate about science, exploration, and making a difference in the world through physics!
