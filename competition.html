Competition Overview

The GPhysics competition is an exciting online physics experience designed to introduce middle school girls (grades 6–8) to the creativity and problem-solving behind real science. It’s free, accessible, and crafted for all experience levels!

Competition Format

  • 📘 Individual Round: 15 short-answer and multiple-choice questions. Topics focus on motion, forces, energy, and simple systems — all within Classical Mechanics. Participants have 50 minutes. No calculator needed.
  • 🧠 Team Round: 3 open-ended questions solved collaboratively with up to 3 teammates. These problems are designed to encourage brainstorming, clear explanations, and real-world thinking. You’ll write and submit typed responses.
  • ⏱️ Relay Round: A fun, fast-paced round where each person solves a problem and passes their result to the next teammate to continue. Accuracy and speed both matter!

Schedule (Eastern Time)

  • 📅 Registration Opens: August 1, 2025

  • 🚀 Competition Day: November 1, 2025

Prizes & Recognition

  • 🏆 Top Scorers: Amazon gift cards + digital certificates
  • 🌟 Best Collaborative Team
  • 🎨 Most Creative Solution (Team Round)
  • 🎓 Bonus: Potential small scholarships or mentorship if sponsored
  • 📜 All participants receive a certificate of participation

Eligibility

  • Open to all girls in grades 6–8, anywhere in the world
  • Only U.S. residents/green card holders are eligible for physical prizes
  • All identities welcome — anyone who identifies as a girl or nonbinary can compete

Rules

  • 🔒 Work must be completed independently in the Individual Round
  • 👩‍👩‍👧‍👧 Teams must consist of registered participants only
  • 📵 No external help, AI tools, or online searches allowed
  • 🧾 We encourage honesty, teamwork, and curiosity

Example Topics

  • Speed, velocity, acceleration
  • Forces (gravity, friction, tension)
  • Energy (kinetic, potential, conservation)
  • Basic motion graphs and reasoning
