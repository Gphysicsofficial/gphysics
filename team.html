Meet the Founder

Jiayu Ellie Su is the founder of GPhysics. She is a passionate student who believes in creating opportunities for young women to discover the excitement and creativity of physics. Ellie created GPhysics to make physics more accessible, fun, and empowering, and to build a community where girls can explore science boldly and collaboratively.

Beyond competitions, Ellie is committed to making physics a welcoming place for everyone — where imagination, curiosity, and perseverance are celebrated just as much as scores and achievements.

Join the Team!

GPhysics is growing! We are looking for volunteers who are passionate about science, education, and creating positive change. If you're interested in helping with:

  • Problem Writing (submit your own original physics problems!)
  • Grading and Solutions Writing
  • Workshops and Pre-Competition Events
  • Community Building and Outreach

We welcome high school students, college students, educators, and anyone excited about encouraging young women in physics. If you would like to join the GPhysics team or contribute original problems, please contact us at gphysics.official@gmail.com!
