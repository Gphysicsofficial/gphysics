Meet the Team

Jiayu Ellie Su is the founder of GPhysics. A student at Phillips Academy with a passion for physics and equity in STEM, Ellie started GPhysics to bring creativity, accessibility, and collaboration to physics competitions for girls.

She has competed in Olympiad-level contests like USAJMO and F=ma, taught STEM workshops for younger students, and believes science is better when everyone is invited in.
