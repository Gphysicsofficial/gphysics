Meet the Team

Jiayu Ellie Su is the founder and president of GPhysics. A student at Phillips Academy with a passion for physics and equity in STEM, Ellie started GPhysics to bring creativity, accessibility, and collaboration to physics competitions for girls.

Including physics, she does a lot of STEM. She has participated in USA(J)MO, MCM, F = MA, USACO, and more!
