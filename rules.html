Competition Rules

Eligibility

  • GPhysics is open to high school girls (grades 9–12) who are U.S. citizens or green card holders.
  • Each participant must register individually and may optionally form a team for team rounds.

Individual Round Rules

  • All individual problems must be completed independently without any external assistance.
  • Participants may not collaborate, seek hints, or use the internet beyond basic calculators and formula sheets if allowed.
  • Participants agree to uphold honesty and academic integrity throughout the competition.

Team Round Rules

  • Teams can have up to 4 members.
  • Team members must be registered individually before forming a team.
  • Teams must only collaborate within their registered members — no external help is allowed.

Guts Round Rules

  • Guts rounds are fast-paced and collaborative. Teams work together under timed conditions to solve problems as they are released.
  • No external tools beyond scratch paper, calculators, and personal teamwork are permitted.

Academic Integrity

GPhysics relies on the honesty and integrity of all participants. By registering, you agree to compete fairly, respect the rules, and uphold the spirit of learning and discovery. Any evidence of cheating, external collaboration, or unfair conduct may result in disqualification.

Other Policies

  • Participants grant GPhysics permission to use anonymized competition statistics for future promotional or educational purposes.
  • Participants will be contacted via email regarding competition details, results, and potential awards.

We believe in celebrating effort, creativity, and resilience — not just scores. Thank you for helping us make GPhysics a supportive and empowering space!
